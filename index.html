What exactly is a philosophy?

Branches, Schools, and Theories

philosophy

When you have a philosophy, you REFLECT on the world around you, see how things fit together, and build a set of beliefs that guides your decision-making.

branches

Humans have been asking questions since the beginning of our history. The four branches help us see how there are different categories of questions.

schools

Paradigms, or schools of philosophy, are imaginary boxes we use to divide humans up into belief camps. Although the divisions are artificial, they help you understand different perspectives.

learning theories

A theory is a strong, well-established way of explaining something that happens in the classsroom. Learning theories help us understand cause and effect in the classroom.
